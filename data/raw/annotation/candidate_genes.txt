# Candidate genes for the diet-methylation-expression concordance analysis.
#
# SOURCE: Klemp et al. 2022, Clinical and Translational Medicine (LIFE-Adult
#   lifestyle-methylation study). Extracted directly from the paper PDF in
#   data/raw/ -- Table 2 (top DMPs, p.6) and Table 3 (top DMRs, p.10).
#
# SCOPE: metabolic-focused subset. The 4 canonical smoking-methylation markers
#   from the tables (AHRR, F2RL3, GFI1, RARA) were EXCLUDED -- the paper flags
#   them as its strongest effects (p < 1e-11) and they are smoking- not diet-
#   driven; our models already adjust for Smoking_score.
#
# CAVEAT: these are Klemp's LIFESTYLE-COMPOSITE (healthy vs unhealthy) hits, used
#   here as an a-priori prior for a DietScore-specific question. They are NOT a
#   diet-specific gene set. Treat the candidate arm as hypothesis-generating.
#
# NOTES: symbols are current HGNC symbols matching Gx_probe_info symbol_INGENUITY;
#   all have >=1 expression probe. KIAA1026 (Table 2) resolved to KAZN via the
#   synonyms_INGENUITY field. One symbol per line; blank lines, full-line and
#   inline (#...) comments are ignored by 03_concordance.R.
